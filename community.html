Alpha Tecnology #9218

The Alpha Technology team was founded in 2019 from the fundamental team that already existed in our unit, Alpha Jac, to participate in the TBR (Brazil robotics tournament) in other categories that were not reached before, called High in this tournament. With the success in this participation, the team was invited to participate in the FTC, thus being the first team in the program in the state of Rio de Janeiro, we participate in this competition until the year 2023, where we were challenged to change to the FRC we are determined to do more success than before, it will be a big challenge, but we are determined to do it.
