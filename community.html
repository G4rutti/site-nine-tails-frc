Community

The Nine Tails #9219 Team understands the importance of meeting new teams. That's why we are in constant contact with other teams, promoting the unity and values ​​of FIRST. Down below are the meetings that we had with other teams and a bit of their history and trajectory.

Here are some teams we talked to throughout our journey:

Alpha Tecnology #9218

The Alpha Technology team was founded in 2019 from the fundamental team that already existed in our unit, Alpha Jac, to participate in the TBR (Brazil robotics tournament) in other categories that were not reached before, called High in this tournament. With the success in this participation, the team was invited to participate in the FTC, thus being the first team in the program in the state of Rio de Janeiro, we participate in this competition until the year 2023, where we were challenged to change to the FRC we are determined to do more success than before, it will be a big challenge, but we are determined to do it.

Megazord #7563

We are Megazord 7563. We were founded in 2018 by young people who believe in changing the world through science and technology. We strive to build technical and non-technical skills among your students through FRC competitions, while inspiring communities to become the next generation of innovation through FIRST and STEAM-based programs that emphasize equal opportunities for all.

Robonáticos #7565

We are Robomáticos 7565, a robotics team present at FRC since 2019, but did you know that our story at FIRST started in 2012. We started at FLL in 2012 and over the years, we have won several achievements, in addition to that in 2015, we were the first team to win the South African International Open. Despite of all the challenges, in 2019 we started at FRC and achieved the feat of being the first Brazilian team to win the Rookie All-Star at the world championship. through that milestone, Robonatics is no longer a team, but an inspiration to young people around the world. Brazil!

TechMaker #9047

Starting out as an FLL team, Techmaker made it big in the Lego world and expanded into FRC and later FTC. With 15 members, the team stands out for being one of the best among beginners, even winning the Rookie All-Star Award 2022 in Rio de Janeiro.

Octopus #7567

The SESI/SENAI Octopus #7567 team emerged in August 2018 from a partnership between two major institutions: SESI and SENAI. It is the first FRC team in the city of Bauru-SP and it is the first Brazilian team to win the Rookie All Star, at the World Festival, in Houston (Texas, USA) in 2019. They defined the octopus as the team's visual identity, because, thus as they have several tentacles and together they form a unique and powerful mind, ready to face challenges and solve problems. Already the color, purple, means creativity and release of fears for problem solving. Currently, FRC Octopus is in its fifth formation. The team aims to include young people in the world of possibilities offered by STEAM, impacting not only them, but society as a whole.

Red Sharks #9199

The Sharks team was born in 2018, after the merger of the SESI units in the city of Taubaté and Tremembé, the union that brought the teams of these units new ideas, determination and companionship! Currently our team participates in OBR, FLL and the new modality, FRC.

Northstar #9302

We are the Northstar #9302 team, the first team from Pará to compete for the First Robotics Competition (FRC) of the SESI Belém school, we are formed by eight members, a mentor and two technicians, our team works on the construction of semiautonomous robots of industrial size, up to 55 kg and 1.5 meters tall, seeking sponsorship, fundraising and social projects. The team works on training young people in the field of robotics, disseminating the FIRST philosophies
