About Us

Our background!

Welcome! This website was created by Nine Tails #9219 team, contestant of FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition), launched and sponsored by FIRST (For Inspiration and Recignition of Science and Technology). The name of the team has been conceived from the asian mythology of the Nine-tailed Fox. It was chosen primarily due to the fox meaning: intelligence, cunning and problem-solving skills, combined with the nine tails representing the team's evolution.

Areas of expertise

Social

The social subteam is composed of the members: Ana Luísa, Mirella de Souza, Rebeca and Vinícios. This area is responsible for the voluntary actions that the team carries out throughout the year and the visual identity of Nine Tails.

Eletrical and Mechanical

The electrical part of the team is made by the members: Davi garutti, Gabriel Vilela and Mirella Rezende. The subteam performs the electrical connections of the robot allowing its operation. The mechanics team are responsible for produce the body of the robot and its chassi. The members are the students: Enzzo Almeida, Gabriel Lauer, Gabriel Rosa and Jessé Nunes.

Programming

The programming members are responsible for controlling the robot's movement systems, in order to unite the work done by the electrical and mechanical teams. The members are: Ana Clara Arruda, Cauã Campos, Erick Maia and Iago Andrade.
